SDG Goal 1: No Poverty

The first Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) aims to **end poverty in all its forms everywhere**. Poverty is a major global issue affecting millions of people. It limits access to essential services such as education, healthcare, and clean water. Many people struggle to meet basic needs and are vulnerable to economic shocks, conflicts, and natural disasters. This goal is essential for ensuring equal opportunities and improving living conditions worldwide.
