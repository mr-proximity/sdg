The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are a set of 17 global goals adopted by the United Nations to address global challenges and promote a sustainable future. These goals aim to tackle issues such as poverty, inequality, climate change, and environmental sustainability, ensuring prosperity for all. The additional Goal 18 represents future sustainability efforts beyond the existing framework.
